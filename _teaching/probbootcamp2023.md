Within this workshop, we recall ideas that are often covered in Probability Theory from multiple perspectives. We aim to finish talking about classical probability results, touching on the main pillars covered in an undergraduate sequence. In addition, we pull back some of the hood and highlight the more rigorous foundations, and keep an eye open towards applications.
